The distance between two vectors is given by 
\begin{align}
\label{1/4/eq5}
\norm{\vec{A}-\vec{B}}&= \sqrt{\myvec{\vec{A}-\vec{B}}^\top\myvec{\vec{A}-\vec{B}}}\\
\end{align}
$\because $
\begin{align}
\vec{A}-\vec{B}&=\myvec{a\\-b}
\\
\norm{\vec{A}-\vec{B}}&=\sqrt{\myvec{a & -b}\myvec{a\\-b}} \\
	&=\sqrt{\myvec{a^2+b^2}} 
\end{align}
